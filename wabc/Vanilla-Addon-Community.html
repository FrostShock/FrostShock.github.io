Vanilla WoW Addon Community

This page is about the Vanilla WoW addon community. This page contains a list of developers and their addons. Most of the addons are hosted on GitHub. Here you can find the homepages of the developers but also the forum threads where each addon was discussed. So you can find more information and you can further discuss those addons.
